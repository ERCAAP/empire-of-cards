# EVENT DESCRIPTIONS

---

## Negative Events (5)

**Health Inspector Visit**
They find the freezer. They always find the freezer. Operation cards generate -20% income this turn while the investigation is open.

**Supplier Dispute**
Your primary supplier is short this week. No explanation, no apology. All ingredient-dependent income reduced by 30% for 2 turns.

**Bad Review Goes Viral**
One unhappy customer, one photo, eleven thousand shares. Platform rating drops immediately. New customer flow halved for 3 turns.

**Staff Walkout**
Three people didn't show up today. They coordinated. All staff capacity reduced by 50% this turn. Morale stays low until you address the cause.

**Competitor Campaign Blitz**
They spent what you save in a month on a single week of ads. Your visible customer share drops by 15% for 2 turns regardless of your marketing spend.

---

## Positive Events (3)

**Viral Trend**
Someone filmed your product. 50,000 views before you knew it was happening. Customer flow +60% for 2 turns. Check your capacity before celebrating.

**Seasonal Rush**
The neighborhood is buying. Every business benefits but the better-prepared one wins more of it. All income +25% this turn.

**Word of Mouth Week**
No campaign. No spend. People are just talking about you. Organic customer gain +4 per turn for 3 turns.

---

## Mixed Events (2)

**Food Festival Weekend**
The whole district is out and hungry. Foot traffic doubles — but so does every competitor on your block. Customer flow +40%, but rival gains the same bonus. Who handles the pressure better decides who keeps them.

**Platform Algorithm Change**
The delivery app updated its ranking system. Some businesses rise, some fall. Your current rating determines whether this is a gift or a penalty. Effects last 3 turns — improve your rating now or brace for reduced visibility.
