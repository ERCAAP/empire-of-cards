What is this game?

Strategy with readable stakes and a tabletop pulse.

Empire of Cards is a strategy game where cards are your businesses, employees, upgrades, and one-turn power plays. You are not crawling a dungeon. You are building a weird little empire and trying to outgrow a rival before they lock up the board.

The fantasy is simple: make strong placements, trigger sharper synergies, survive the events, and claim the market one district at a time.

The fast pitch

A card-driven business strategy game where you chain combos and fight for market share on a living tabletop board.

Founder note

Built as a clean restart.

Empire of Cards is being built by a solo developer who spent years shipping and optimizing games played by millions, then went back to first principles to make a sharper, stranger strategy game of his own.

The goal is not to make a spreadsheet with cards. It is to make a readable, juicy, competitive board where every smart setup feels like a takeover.

FAQ

Questions players will ask first.

What kind of game is Empire of Cards?

It is a strategy game where cards become businesses, employees, upgrades, and one-turn plays. Your objective is to outbuild a rival and control the board’s market share before they do.

Is this launching on mobile and web too?

The current focus is PC via Steam. If the game lands well, other platforms can be explored later, but they are not the first promise.
